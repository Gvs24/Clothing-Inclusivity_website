Stores with More Specific Sizes

For a Perfect Fit

Some stores provide more than just Small, Medium, and Large. Check out these stores that offer specific size options for better fits.

Stores Offering Detailed Size Options

  • ASOS – Offers a range of sizes, including tall and petite categories for both men and women. Pricing: Moderate to Pricey.
  • Nordstrom – Known for carrying a variety of sizes, including petite, plus-size, and tall options. Pricing: Moderate to Pricey.
  • J.Crew – Offers extended sizes, with options like petite, tall, and plus sizes for both men and women. Pricing: Moderate to Pricey.
  • Target – Provides size-inclusive options with affordable prices. Pricing: Cheap to Moderate.
  • Madewell – Known for casual wear, offering a range of sizes including petite, tall, and plus sizes. Pricing: Moderate.
  • Old Navy – Offers a wide selection of sizes, from regular to plus sizes. Pricing: Cheap to Moderate.
  • Uniqlo – Features inclusive sizing, including extra small and tall options. Pricing: Cheap to Moderate.
  • Lululemon – Provides a wide range of sizes for athletic wear, including petite and tall options. Pricing: Pricey.
  • Fabletics – Known for size-inclusive athletic wear with comfortable fits for all body types. Pricing: Moderate.
